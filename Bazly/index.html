WHAT IS CODING.BN?

Coding.bn is a 6-month course program initiated by AITI Brunei to train unemployed youths with coding/programming skills aligned with demand in the industry. During these 6 months course and mentorship program, participants will have the oppotunity to undertake apprenticeship in companies to work on real projects allowing them to experience a real working environment!

In the Coding Academy, we will teach you more than just coding skills. You will learn how to solve problems and be a self-sufficient developer.
